Roadmap

  • November & December 2021

    Project Conception

  • January & February 2022

    Development & Marketing

  • February 2022

    Whitelist

    At least 500 whitelist positions will be available. 1 Swole Ape mint per whitelist. Whitelisters will also receive a slightly discounted price.

  • March 2022

    Pre-Sale & Launch

    Our first launch will include 3,333 unique Swole Apes, which after the whitelist pre-sale, leaves 2,833 at launch.

  • April & May 2022

    DAO Launch on Avalanche

    Airdrop & Whitelist for Swole Ape NFT holders to ensure priority in governance. Pre-sale to establish liquidity, followed by public sale & launch.

  • Q2 & Q3 2022

    Determine Gym Location & Begin Working

    Governance vote in which the first Swole Ape Gym location is determined. LA, NYC, and Miami are current top contenders.

  • Q2 2023

    Gym Grand Opening
